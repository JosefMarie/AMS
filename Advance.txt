Based on the current architecture of the AMS (Academy Management System), here are some strategic suggestions for each role and the system as a whole:

1. For Administrators (The Control Center)
System Configuration UI: Instead of editing settings.py or templates for minor changes, add a "Settings" page to manage:
Feature Toggles: (e.g., Enable/Disable AI Quizzer, Public Registration).
Branding: Upload a logo or change primary "Theme Colors" directly from the dashboard.
Audit Logging: A dedicated "Security Logs" view that tracks who did what (e.g., "Admin X deleted Trainer Y at 10:30 AM").
Visual Analytics (Chart.js): Replace static numbers with interactive charts:
Performance trends (which modules have the highest/lowest average scores).
Enrollment growth over the last 6 months.
2. For Trainers (Empowering Educators)
Classroom Broadcasts: A "News & Announcements" feature where trainers can post messages visible only to their specific students.
Bulk Grade Import: Allow trainers to upload a CSV file of marks to update student profiles instantly.
Interactive Gradebook: A spreadsheet-like view where they can see all students in a class alongside all their module scores in one large grid.
3. For Students (Student Success)
Learning Journey Graph: A visual timeline showing their progress through the curriculum—which modules are "Mastered," "In Progress," or "Locked."
Portfolio Profile: Allow students to set a profile picture, write a bio, and add their "Top Skills" based on their highest-scoring modules (great for employment).
Resource Library: A section where they can download PDFs or watch videos uploaded by their trainers for each module.
4. For the Whole App (App-Wide Innovation)
Dark Mode Toggle: A "Premium" app isn't complete without a sleek Dark/Night mode that switches all glassmorphism effects to deep grays and neon indigo.
Progressive Web App (PWA): Allow users to "Install" the AMS on their home screens (mobile or desktop) with offline support for basic viewing.
AI Education Assistant: Integrate a "Teaching Assistant" AI that can:
Analyze a student's weak points and suggest which module to study next.
Help trainers generate lesson plan descriptions or quiz questions automatically.
Notification System: Real-time in-app alerts (toast notifications) when marks are entered, attendance is marked, or a password is changed.
