Step 1
Home Base

Your Student Dashboard

When you log in, you land on your personal dashboard. It shows your profile progress, any teacher announcements, and quick-links to your modules, report, and AI assistant.

👤 Profile Section

Your name, ID, level, and profile completeness percentage are shown here.

📢 Announcements

Your teacher's broadcast messages appear here — never miss an important update.

⚡ Quick Links

Direct access cards to your Learning Journey, AI Assistant, and Portfolio.

Go to Dashboard
Step 2
Visual Progress

Interactive Learning Journey

The Learning Journey is your visual progress map. It shows all your enrolled modules as cards with progress bars indicating your mastery level based on your assessment scores across all terms.

How to navigate

Sidebar → "Learning Journey". Each module card shows your current score average and a colour-coded progress bar (red → amber → green).

Click a module

Tap any module card to open the full session plan detail — showing your chronological marks across ALL terms.

Open Learning Journey
Step 3
Marks History New

Cross-Term Marks & Score Breakdown

When you open a module from your Learning Journey, you'll see a premium marks card that lists every assessment mark you've received — across all terms and academic years. Marks are sorted chronologically and include type badges (FA/IA/SA), score bars, and an overall average.

FA

Formative Assessment

IA

Internal Assessment

SA

Summative Assessment

Tip: Even if a session plan's module field doesn't exactly match your assessment, the system uses smart keyword matching to find all your relevant marks.

Step 5
Reports & Portfolio

Academic Report & Portfolio

Download your full academic transcript as a polished PDF from "My Report" button on the dashboard. Your Portfolio showcases your achievements and is shareable via a public link.

📄 Academic Report

Dashboard → "My Report" → Download PDF. Includes all marks, attendance, and assessment details per module.

🌟 Portfolio

Sidebar → "Portfolio". A public-facing page of your academic achievements with a shareable URL.

Student Pro Tips

  • → Complete your profile (add email) so you receive mark notifications and can reset passwords.
  • → Check "Learning Journey" after every assessment to track your progress in real-time.
  • → Use the AI assistant weekly — it picks up on your weakest topics automatically.
  • → Download your transcript PDF at the end of each term to keep personal records.
