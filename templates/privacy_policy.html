01 Introduction & Governing Law

Welcome to the **Academic Management System (AMS)**. We operate as a secure educational data controller and process personal information strictly in compliance with the **Law No. 058/2021 of 13/10/2021** relating to the Protection of Personal Data and Privacy in the Republic of Rwanda.

This policy outlines the types of data we gather, our processing practices, system-wide security architectures, and the legal channels provided for you to manage, restrict, or download your personal information.

02 Personal Data We Collect

AMS processes specific educational and administrative indicators needed to deliver student portfolios, transcripts, and curriculum structures. We gather the following data points:

Personal Profile Data

Full names, institutional email addresses, role identifiers (Student, Trainer, Admin), phone numbers, and optional profile pictures.

Academic Records

Competency evaluations, trade associations, modules, attendance records, Formative and Summative Assessment marks, and transcript histories.

AI Processing Metadata

Syllabus documents, lesson objectives, and query vectors passed to the AI Advisor for pedagogical planning and grade summaries.

System Auditing Logs

Timestamped login events, IP addresses, session duration, and administrative activity logs to secure internal portals.

03 Purposes of Data Processing

We collect and process your personal data solely for legitimate educational, regulatory, and administrative operations:

  • **Educational Management**: Calculating term-specific grade percentages, mapping attendance thresholds, and tracking cumulative GPA.
  • **AI-Guided Facilitation**: Analyzing syllabus structures to generate custom lesson plans, quizzes, and learning objectives for trainers.
  • **Security Verification**: Generating real-time audit trails of user logins, role switches, and grade changes to prevent unauthorized modifications.

04 Security & Encryption Protocols

Data safety is a core architectural pillar of AMS. We implement robust defense-in-depth measures to secure the platform's infrastructure:

SSL Encryption & Serverless Isolation

All database streams connecting to our cloud storage endpoints are isolated over TLS/SSL tunnels using encrypted poolers. Passwords utilize cryptographic pbkdf2 hashing, while strict role restrictions ensure trainers, students, and administrators only access scoped databases.

05 Data Retention Schedule

Academic records are maintained based on educational compliance guidelines:

  • Active Profiles: Kept for the duration of a user's enrollment or employment.
  • Academic History: Marks, competencies, and achievements are stored permanently to facilitate future transcript verifications.
  • Security Audit Logs: Automatically deleted or archived after 12 months.

06 Your Legal Rights (Law No. 058/2021)

Under Rwandan Personal Data Protection regulations, you hold explicit rights to control your profile:

Access & Portability

You may request a copy of all accumulated marks, personal info, and attendance charts in readable formats.

Correction & Erasure

Request correction of grade discrepancies or the deletion of optional biographical profile features.

Objection & Restriction

You have the right to withdraw optional consent for secondary educational newsletter or profile metrics.
