Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Academic Management System (AMS). We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information generated within our TVET training portal.

2. Data We Collect

To provide high-quality educational tracking, we collect the following:

  • Personal Profiles: Names, email addresses, and identification numbers.
  • Academic Records: Attendance logs, assessment marks, and competency progress.
  • System Logs: Login history and activity logs for security auditing.
  • Demographic Info: Gender/Sex for institutional reporting purposes.

3. Purpose of Processing

Your data is processed strictly for educational and administrative purposes:

  • Generating student transcripts and performance reports.
  • Ensuring compliance with attendance requirements.
  • Optimizing facilitation through AI-generated session plans.
  • Maintaining a secure environment for all users.

4. Data Access & Security

AMS uses industry-standard encryption and access controls. Data is restricted based on user roles (Trainers see their classes; Students see only their personal records).

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your data or this policy, please contact our System Administrator.
